storey's signature portrait process

Four Easy Steps to Book Your Portrait Session

  1. Request a portrait session by email or phone.
  2. Book a complimentary virtual ordering appointment for two days after your portrait.
  3. Sign and return the contract (adding locations, details and preferences).
  4. Pay by eTransfer. That's It!

Four Easy Steps to Order During Your Sales Session

  1. Preview images with me during our virtual sales session by Zoom (two days after portrait session).
  2. Select your favourite images and assign a product to each of your selections (digital files, framed prints, albums, wall art, greeting cards, etc.).
  3. Finalize your order during the sales session. For totals greater than your print credit, pay by eTransfer.
  4. Products will be shipped directly to you when ready. If you ordered digital downloads they will be sent to you by email.

From Capture to Print
